🎯 The Goal

Test your knowledge of sustainability and energy to climb the ladder. Answer 20 questions to maximize your winnings and aim for the grand prize of 1,000,000 Points!

⚙️ How It Works

  • 20 Questions: Each game includes 20 questions. You will answer all 20 regardless of whether your answers are correct or incorrect.
  • Climb the Ladder: Correct answers move you UP the ladder. Wrong answers keep you at the same level — you won't fall back!
  • Difficulty by Level: Your prize level determines the question difficulty! Levels 0-7 feature EASY questions, 8-14 feature MEDIUM questions, and 15-20 feature HARD questions. Keep winning to face harder questions!
  • Beat the Clock: You have 30 seconds to answer each question. If the timer runs out, it counts as a wrong answer!
  • Milestones: Key milestones at 8,000 Points and 150,000 Points mark your progress!

🆘 Helplines

You get one of each helpline per game. Use them wisely!

  • Elimination: Removes one random incorrect answer to improve your odds.
  • Add Time: Adds an extra 30 seconds to the clock when you need more time to think.
  • Skip Question: Skips the current question and moves you to the next one without losing progress.

🏆 Winning & Glory

After your game ends, you can:

  • Download a Certificate: Receive a personalized certificate to celebrate the number of Points you've won.
  • Join the Leaderboard: If your score ranks in the Top 15, you can add your initials to the leaderboard for everyone to see! Your total time is used as a tiebreaker, so answer quickly and accurately!

Terms & Conditions

This game is intended for educational and entertainment purposes only. Points are a virtual currency within the game and hold no real-world monetary value.
