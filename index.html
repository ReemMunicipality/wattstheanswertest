🎯 The Goal

Test your knowledge on sustainability and energy to climb the money ladder. Answer 20 questions to maximize your winnings and aim for the grand prize of 1,000,000 Reem Coins!

⚙️ How It Works

  • 20 Questions: Each game has 20 questions. You'll answer all 20 regardless of right or wrong!
  • Climb the Ladder: Correct answers move you UP the ladder. Wrong answers keep you at the same level — no falling back!
  • Difficulty by Level: Your prize level determines question difficulty! Levels 0-7 give EASY questions, 8-14 give MEDIUM, and 15-20 give HARD. Keep winning to face harder questions!
  • Beat the Clock: You have 30 seconds to answer each question. If the timer runs out, it counts as a wrong answer!
  • Milestones: Key milestones at 8,000 Reem Coins and 150,000 Reem Coins mark your progress!
  • Fresh Questions: Questions you've seen won't repeat for 5 games, keeping each playthrough fresh!

🆘 Lifelines

You get one of each lifeline per game. Use them wisely!

  • Elimination: Removes one random incorrect answer to improve your odds.
  • Add Time: Adds an extra 30 seconds to the clock when you need more time to think.
  • Skip Question: Skip the current question and move to the next one without losing progress.

🏆 Winning & Glory

After your game ends, you can:

  • Download a Certificate: Get a personalized certificate to celebrate the amount of Reem Coins you've won.
  • Join the Leaderboard: If your score is high enough to make the Top 15, you can add your initials to the leaderboard for all to see! Your total time is used as a tie-breaker, so answer quickly and accurately!

Terms & Conditions

This game is intended for educational and entertainment purposes only. The Reem Coins (RC) are a virtual currency within the game and hold no real-world monetary value.
