🎯 The Goal

Answer all questions correctly for a chance to win the grand prize of 1,000,000 points.

🎮 How It Works

You will face 20 questions in each game and progress through all of them.

  • You move through all questions in order
  • The challenge continues until all 20 are attempted

🪜 Climb the Ladder

  • Correct answers move you up the ladder
  • Wrong answers keep you at the same level
  • You will not lose progress or move backward

⏱ Beat the Clock

  • You have 30 seconds per question
  • If time runs out, it counts as a wrong answer

📊 Difficulty by Level

Questions get harder the more correct answers you give:

  • First 8 correct answers → Easy
  • Next 7 correct answers → Medium
  • Last 5 correct answers → Hard

A wrong answer keeps you at the same level, so the difficulty only goes up when you answer correctly.

🧩 Helplines

Each game includes one use per helpline. Use them wisely:

Elimination – removes one wrong option
Add Time – adds +30 seconds
Skip Question – moves to next question with no penalty

🏆 Winning & Glory

After the game, you can:

  • Download a personalised certificate based on your score
  • Join the leaderboard if you're in the Top 15
  • Tiebreaker is based on total time (faster = better rank)

📜 Terms & Conditions

This game is intended for educational and entertainment purposes only.

Points have no real-world monetary value.
